## Purpose

RecordArr owns documents, records, evidence, files, scans, OCR/extraction metadata, controlled document lifecycle, evidence packages, retention, legal hold, and audit-ready record packages for STL Compliance / ARR.

RecordArr answers:

- What file/document/record exists?
- What version is current?
- What product/object caused the record?
- What evidence package does this record belong to?
- What OCR/extracted metadata exists?
- Is this document approved, superseded, expired, archived, or under legal hold?
- What retention policy applies?
- Can this record be used as evidence?
- Which products are linked to this evidence?

RecordArr does not own operational decisions, regulatory meaning, quality release decisions, training completion truth, asset truth, inventory truth, route truth, order truth, customer truth, supplier truth, or accounting execution.
