# MaintainArr — Reservations, Readiness, Voice Inspection, and Parts Intelligence

## Asset reservation and motor pool

Support request, approval, assignment, checkout, handoff, return, cancellation/no-show, utilization, chargeback snapshot, and conflict handling. Reservation feasibility consumes MaintainArr readiness, StaffArr authority/assignment, TrainArr qualifications, RoutArr commitments, and active holds without copying their truth.

## Readiness

Expose plain-language available/watched/blocked decisions with defects, overdue PM, inspections, qualifications, assignments, and evidence. Overrides require permission, reason, expiry, and audit.

## Voice-guided inspection

Support TTS/STT-guided steps, pause/resume/break, timestamps, media/evidence, operator complaint, duration anomalies, and safe manual fallback. The server owns final step/signoff state.

## Parts intelligence

MaintainArr may provide first-class provider lookups such as O'Reilly through controlled integrations, while SupplyArr owns supplier/procurement truth and LoadArr owns inventory. Part selection stores canonical/vendor references and fitment snapshots; pricing/availability snapshots are dated.

## UI

Remove raw diagnostic JSON from ordinary views, replace browser dialogs with shared dialogs, and use guided asset create with required basics first and optional backfill later.
