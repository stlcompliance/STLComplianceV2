# CustomArr — CRM Pipeline, Service, and Portal

## CRM completeness

CustomArr owns tenant customer truth plus leads/prospects, accounts, contacts, locations, relationships, opportunities, activities, communications, consent/preferences, contracts/requirements, onboarding, risk/holds, service cases, portal identities/scopes, and customer-facing history.

## Pipeline

Use configurable stages with server transitions, probability/forecast snapshots, next action, owner/team, products/services of interest, proposal/quote references, loss reasons, and activity timeline. Conversion to customer/order creates owner-backed records without duplicate local identity.

## Portal

Customers may submit requests/orders, documents, approvals, status questions, and issue/service cases only within invitation/account scope. OrdArr owns resulting order orchestration; CustomArr preserves customer context and portal conversation.

## Quality and tests

Split monolithic UI into route/domain modules. Add frontend/E2E coverage for dedupe/merge, contact/location updates, portal visibility, consent, opportunity conversion, quick create, and OrdArr handoff.
