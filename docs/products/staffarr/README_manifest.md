## Purpose

StaffArr owns the people, organization, permissions, internal locations, personnel incidents, readiness view, and person history layer for STL Compliance / ARR.

StaffArr answers:

- Who is this person?
- Where do they belong in the organization?
- What site, department, team, position, and manager are they tied to?
- What internal locations exist?
- What product permissions and role assignments do they have?
- What is their operational readiness?
- What personnel incidents, restrictions, overrides, and history exist?
- What can other products safely reference when assigning work?

StaffArr does not own platform login, product entitlement, training/certification truth, maintenance truth, inventory truth, procurement truth, route execution, customer order truth, document file truth, quality hold truth, regulatory meaning, reporting read models, or accounting execution.
