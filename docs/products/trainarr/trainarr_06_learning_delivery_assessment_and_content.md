# TrainArr — Learning Delivery, Assessment, and Content

## LMS completeness

TrainArr supports versioned programs, modules, steps, prerequisites, assignments, cohorts, instructor-led sessions, self-paced delivery, observations, knowledge/skills assessments, accommodations, remediation, renewals, certificates, and qualification decisions.

## Program builder

Use a guided builder with Overview, Audience & Applicability, Modules & Steps, Assessments, Signoffs, Certificates/Qualifications, Renewal/Remediation, Citations/Evidence, and Publish Review. Draft versions are editable; activated versions are immutable/superseded.

## Assessments

Question banks, randomized forms, scoring, attempt policy, practical skill rubrics, evaluator signoff, and appeal/review are explicit. AI may propose content but cannot silently publish or determine qualification.

## Delivery UX

Learners see assigned, due, overdue, blocked, in-progress, completed, and renewal work. Instructors/evaluators receive queues and clear evidence/signoff requirements. Field Companion may execute steps offline but TrainArr confirms final state.
