# RoutArr — Dispatch Workspace, Navigation, and Execution

## Unified dispatch workspace

Combine demand, planning suggestions, unassigned work, driver/equipment capacity, active trips, exceptions, yard/gate status, and appointment risk without turning the sidebar into every operational leaf.

## Navigation

Primary groups: Plan, Dispatch, Execute/Track, Yard & Appointments, Freight/Claims, Analysis, Administration. High-frequency board views are routes; record-specific work is tabs/drawers.

## Drag/drop scheduling

Drag/drop is a proposal until the server validates driver HOS/qualification, equipment readiness, appointment windows, customer requirements, capacity, holds, and concurrency. Conflicts open a structured drawer; no visual move is final until accepted.

## Execution truth

Replace browser confirms with shared dialogs. Preserve user work on conflict/unavailable integration. Validate demand-to-trip, tender, rating, telematics, detention, claims, and finance-packet scenarios through cross-product E2E and performance tests.
