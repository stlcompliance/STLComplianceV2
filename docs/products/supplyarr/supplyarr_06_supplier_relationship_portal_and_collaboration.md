# SupplyArr — Supplier Relationship, Portal, and Collaboration

## SRM scope

Support supplier onboarding, qualification, contacts/sites, category ownership, sourcing events, bids/quotes, contracts, price/lead-time snapshots, scorecards, risk, corrective actions, document requirements, diversity/classification, performance reviews, and relationship plans.

## External collaboration

Vendor/supplier portal sessions are invitation- and scope-limited. External actors may acknowledge POs, update promised dates, submit shipment/ASN data, upload evidence, respond to quality actions, and confirm completion only for explicitly shared records.

## Completion and dispatch

Vendor completion can create or update an OrdArr/RoutArr demand/handoff through durable idempotent contracts. SupplyArr owns supplier/procurement truth; RoutArr owns transport execution; LoadArr owns receiving; AssurArr owns quality disposition.

## UI

Supplier detail uses Overview, Contacts & Locations, Items/Services, Sourcing & Contracts, Orders, Compliance & Quality, Performance, Documents, Activity/History. Cross-product values are live references and snapshots are dated.
