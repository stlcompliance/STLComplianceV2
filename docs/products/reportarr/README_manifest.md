## Purpose

ReportArr owns the cross-suite reporting, dashboards, analytics, scheduled reports, exports, audit reporting, KPI definitions, and reporting read models for STL Compliance / ARR.

ReportArr answers:

- What happened across the suite?
- What KPIs are trending up or down?
- What reports can be generated?
- What dashboards exist?
- What source products contributed to this report?
- How fresh is this data?
- What scheduled reports should run?
- What export was generated?
- What operational facts should executives, managers, auditors, supervisors, and workers see?

ReportArr does not own operational truth. Source products remain authoritative.
