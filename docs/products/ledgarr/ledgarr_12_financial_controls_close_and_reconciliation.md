# LedgArr — Financial Controls, Close, and Reconciliation

## Audit mandate

Financial records require stricter invariants than ordinary operational records: balanced journals, immutable posting, closed-period controls, durable packet idempotency, concurrency, approval segregation, and reversal instead of destructive edit.

## Close workspace

Provide close calendar, task/checklist ownership, subledger readiness, unposted packets, reconciliations, exceptions, approvals, period lock, reopen workflow, supporting evidence, and close package generation.

## Reconciliation

Support bank, AR, AP, inventory valuation, intercompany, fixed asset, tax, and control-account reconciliation with source balance, ledger balance, variance, aging, evidence, preparer/reviewer, and resolution status.

## Navigation

- Finance Operations: Dashboard, Journal, AP, AR, Payments
- Inventory and Assets: Valuation, Fixed Assets, Projects
- Planning: Budgets, Forecasts
- Close and Reporting: Period Close, Reconciliation, Statements, Audit
- Administration: Legal Entities, Chart of Accounts, Dimensions, Integrations, Settings, Permissions

FinancialLegalEntity remains distinct from Compliance Core GoverningBody in API names and UI labels.
