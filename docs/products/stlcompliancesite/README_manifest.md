## Purpose

STLComplianceSite owns the public website, public product messaging, public suite explanation, legal/trust pages, founder/origin story, lead intake, demo requests, and marketing content for STL Compliance.

It does not own authenticated product operations, customer operational records, CRM truth after handoff, product entitlement, product login, support ticket truth, billing/accounting execution, or product-domain data.
