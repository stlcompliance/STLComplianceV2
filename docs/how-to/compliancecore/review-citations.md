# How to review citations

## Audience
Compliance admins, auditors, and rule reviewers

## Product
Compliance Core

## Support Status
Supported by current UI/API

## Purpose
Find and review legal or policy citations used by Compliance Core mappings.

## Before You Start
- Compliance Core owns citations and compliance rule mapping.
- Operational products should reference Compliance Core rule meaning rather than copying citation logic.

## Steps
1. Open Compliance Core.
2. Open Mappings or Citations.
3. Search or filter by citation, requirement, governing body, rulepack, or status.
4. Open the citation detail.
5. Review related requirements, evidence types, evidence requirements, applicability, and product mappings.
6. Use Findings or Evaluation to understand impact when needed.
7. Update mapping only through Compliance Core controls.

## What Happens Next
Citations support rule evaluation, evidence expectations, audit packages, and compliance reports.

## Troubleshooting
- If a citation does not match a requirement, review rulepack and mapping state.
- If operational evidence is missing, correct evidence capture in the owning product or RecordArr.
