# How to sign in to STL Compliance

## Audience
All users

## Product
Platform Access

## Support Status
Supported by current UI/API

## Purpose
Get into the STL Compliance suite through NexArr, the platform login and tenant access authority.

## Before You Start
- You need an active platform user account.
- If your tenant requires verification or MFA, complete those prompts before launching products.

## Steps
1. Open the STL Compliance sign-in page.
2. Enter your email and password.
3. Complete any required verification or MFA prompt.
4. Confirm the tenant shown after sign-in is the tenant you intend to work in.
5. Launch the product you need from the suite workspace or product switcher.

## What Happens Next
NexArr validates your identity, tenant membership, and product entitlement before any product opens. Product-specific permissions are still checked inside each product.

## Troubleshooting
- If sign-in fails, ask a platform admin to verify your user is active and not locked.
- If a product is missing after sign-in, confirm the tenant has the product entitlement and that your user has access for that product.
