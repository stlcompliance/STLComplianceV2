# How to remove or deactivate access

## Audience
Platform administrators, tenant administrators, StaffArr permission administrators, and authorized managers

## Purpose
Remove authority or login access without deleting business history.

## Steps
1. For employment/contractor status changes, start the offboarding or status workflow in StaffArr.
2. Remove or expire role/permission assignments and scoped responsibilities that should end.
3. Remove active tenant membership when the person should no longer act for that tenant.
4. Disable or lock the NexArr account only when the person should not sign in anywhere.
5. Revoke active sessions for immediate effect.
6. Preserve person, audit, evidence, approval, and execution history.
7. Review downstream task reassignment, legal hold, records custody, and approval delegation.

Do not look for a per-product launch gate. To remove one category of work, remove the corresponding product permissions or scoped assignments.
