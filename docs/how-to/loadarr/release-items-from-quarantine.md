# How to release items from quarantine

## Audience
Warehouse supervisors, quality users, and inventory admins

## Product
LoadArr

## Support Status
Supported by current UI/API

## Purpose
Release held inventory after review, correction, or approval.

## Before You Start
- The hold must exist in LoadArr.
- Any required quality, compliance, vendor, or supervisor review should be completed before release.

## Steps
1. Open LoadArr.
2. Open Holds or Inventory.
3. Find the held item, lot, serial, or receipt line.
4. Review the hold reason, source reference, quantity, and evidence.
5. Choose Release hold.
6. Enter release quantity, reason, and approval notes when requested.
7. Submit the release.
8. Confirm the released quantity is available or moved to the correct next location.
9. Review access or movement history when audit evidence is needed.

## What Happens Next
LoadArr updates hold status and inventory availability. Supporting quality or compliance records remain owned by their products.

## Troubleshooting
- If only part of the quantity can be released, enter the partial quantity and leave the remainder on hold if the UI supports it.
- If release is blocked, verify required approvals or related quality/compliance reviews.
