# How to stage received items

## Audience
Warehouse receivers and inventory coordinators

## Product
LoadArr

## Support Status
Supported by current UI/API

## Purpose
Hold received goods in a controlled staging state before final putaway or issue.

## Before You Start
- Receiving must be started or completed in LoadArr.
- The staging location should be a valid StaffArr location or LoadArr warehouse area reference, depending on configuration.

## Steps
1. Open LoadArr.
2. Open Receiving or Staging.
3. Select the received item, receipt line, or handling unit.
4. Confirm quantity, lot, serial, and current receiving state.
5. Choose the staging action available in the page.
6. Select the staging area or dock location.
7. Save the staging move.
8. Review the item in Staging before putaway, transfer, hold, or issue.

## What Happens Next
LoadArr records the staging movement and keeps stock traceability through receiving and movement history.

## Troubleshooting
- If the staging location is missing, check StaffArr location setup and LoadArr location rules.
- If the item is on hold, release or resolve the hold before putting it away.
