# How to put away inventory

## Audience
Warehouse receivers and inventory coordinators

## Product
LoadArr

## Support Status
Supported by current UI/API

## Purpose
Move received or staged inventory into its controlled storage location.

## Before You Start
- Receiving or staging should already be recorded in LoadArr.
- The destination must be allowed by LoadArr location rules.

## Steps
1. Open LoadArr.
2. Open Putaway.
3. Select the receipt line, item, or handling unit waiting for putaway.
4. Review item, quantity, lot, serial, current location, and suggested destination if shown.
5. Choose or confirm the storage location.
6. Enter the quantity being put away.
7. Submit the putaway action.
8. Confirm the item appears in Inventory at the destination location.

## What Happens Next
LoadArr records the inventory movement and updates balances and movement history.

## Troubleshooting
- If the destination is blocked, check location rules, holds, item policy, and lot/serial requirements.
- If the item needs quarantine, create a hold instead of putting it into available stock.
