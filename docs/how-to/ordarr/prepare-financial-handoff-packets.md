# How to prepare financial handoff packets

## Audience
Order coordinators, billing preparation users, and managers

## Product
OrdArr

## Support Status
Supported by product contract/docs

## Purpose
Prepare invoice-ready and bill-ready packets without making OrdArr the accounting system.

## Before You Start
- Order is closed or completion-ready.
- Required completion evidence and execution summaries are available.
- Finance handoff process is approved for the tenant.

## Steps
1. Open OrdArr.
2. Open the order request.
3. Open Completion packets, Invoice-ready packets, or Bill-ready packets.
4. Review customer context, order lines, completion evidence, execution summaries, and packet warnings.
5. Resolve missing or conflicting packet data.
6. Prepare or refresh the packet.
7. Export or hand off the packet only through the approved finance integration or manual finance process.

## What Happens Next
OrdArr owns packet preparation and status. External finance systems own invoices, bills, payments, tax, and general ledger posting.

## Troubleshooting
- If packet totals or references are incomplete, review order lines and execution summaries.
- If accounting status is needed, check the finance system.
