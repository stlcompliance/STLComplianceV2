# How to export a report

## Audience
Report users, managers, auditors, and admins

## Product
ReportArr

## Support Status
Supported by current UI/API

## Purpose
Export a report or dashboard artifact for sharing, audit, or offline review.

## Before You Start
- You need permission to export the report.
- Do not export sensitive data unless you have a valid business reason and approval.
- RecordArr may store report artifacts when they need retention.

## Steps
1. Open ReportArr.
2. Open Reports or Dashboards.
3. Open the report or dashboard.
4. Apply filters first so the export includes the intended scope.
5. Choose the export action.
6. Select an allowed export format.
7. Confirm the export request.
8. Open Exports to monitor status or download the artifact.
9. Store or attach the exported artifact through RecordArr when retention or audit control is required.

## What Happens Next
ReportArr creates the export artifact and export history. Source products remain unchanged.

## Troubleshooting
- If export is disabled, verify report export permission and report access policy.
- If the export is too broad, narrow filters before exporting.
