# How to create a site

## Audience
StaffArr admins and facilities or operations managers

## Product
StaffArr

## Support Status
Supported by current UI/API

## Purpose
Create an internal site such as a facility, yard, office, or operating campus.

## Before You Start
- StaffArr owns internal sites and operational locations.
- You need StaffArr location administration access.

## Steps
1. Open StaffArr.
2. Open Organization Structure or Locations.
3. Choose the site area.
4. Create the site with its name, address or operating description, status, and parent context if the page asks for one.
5. Save the site.
6. Add buildings, rooms, docks, yards, or other locations under the site when needed.
7. Confirm consuming products use this StaffArr location reference.

## What Happens Next
The site becomes the canonical internal place used by maintenance, warehouse, dispatch, training, and compliance workflows.

## Troubleshooting
- If a bin or warehouse address is treated as an internal physical location, define the place in StaffArr and let LoadArr own inventory movement inside it.
- If the site is a customer location, do not make StaffArr the customer master; use the customer-owning product when available.
