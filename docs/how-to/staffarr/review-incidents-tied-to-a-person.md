# How to review incidents tied to a person

## Audience
Managers, HR, safety users, and StaffArr admins

## Product
StaffArr

## Support Status
Supported by current UI/API

## Purpose
Review a person incident history alongside readiness, restrictions, and training context.

## Before You Start
- You need permission to view the person and incident history.
- StaffArr owns the personnel incident view even when another product owns the original operational event.

## Steps
1. Open StaffArr.
2. Open People and find the person.
3. Open the person detail page.
4. Review the incidents, readiness, restrictions, certifications, and history areas.
5. Open an incident to see narrative, severity, involved people, related records, evidence references, and workflow state.
6. Use TrainArr or the originating product to correct the source workflow when the incident points to training, dispatch, maintenance, receiving, or another lane.
7. Use RecordArr for supporting documents and audit packages.

## What Happens Next
StaffArr provides the person-centered incident record and history. Other products remain the source of truth for their operational records.

## Troubleshooting
- If the incident is missing, search StaffArr Incidents directly and confirm the person is listed as involved.
- If training status is stale, check TrainArr assignment or qualification publication.
