# How to create departments, positions, and teams

## Audience
StaffArr admins and department leaders

## Product
StaffArr

## Support Status
Supported by current UI/API

## Purpose
Build the work-authority structure used by people, managers, and permission assignments.

## Before You Start
- StaffArr owns departments, positions, teams, reporting structure, and manager relationships.
- You need StaffArr organization or role administration access.

## Steps
1. Open StaffArr.
2. Open Organization Structure.
3. Choose the area for departments, positions, or teams.
4. Create the needed record using the page create panel.
5. Connect it to the correct parent organization unit, site, or manager context.
6. Set the active status.
7. Save the record.
8. Assign people or permissions to the new structure only after confirming the ownership and reporting relationships are correct.

## What Happens Next
Departments, positions, and teams can drive readiness, permission scope, assignments, and reporting across products.

## Troubleshooting
- If a product needs a role or team for authorization, create the people structure in StaffArr and configure product permission scope there.
- If the UI label differs, use the StaffArr Organization Structure section that matches the record type.
