# How to handle a training-related incident

## Audience
Managers, safety users, HR, and training coordinators

## Product
StaffArr

## Support Status
Supported by current UI/API with intended cross-product follow-up

## Purpose
Flag an incident for training review and coordinate follow-up without making StaffArr own the training assignment.

## Before You Start
- StaffArr owns the personnel incident.
- TrainArr owns retraining, assignments, evaluations, and qualification changes.

## Steps
1. Open StaffArr.
2. Open the incident or create a new incident.
3. In the training or certification evaluation area, mark that TrainArr review may be required.
4. Describe the training concern clearly in the incident narrative.
5. Add the affected person and any related source record.
6. Submit or update the incident.
7. Open TrainArr Remediation or Assignments to create or review the retraining work when the TrainArr follow-up is available.
8. Return to StaffArr to monitor the incident and person readiness status.

## What Happens Next
StaffArr records the incident and training concern. TrainArr owns the resulting retraining assignment and completion evidence.

## Troubleshooting
- If no TrainArr assignment appears, check whether the remediation handoff or manual assignment step has been implemented for the tenant.
- Do not close the incident solely because retraining was requested; verify completion or the applicable follow-up state.
