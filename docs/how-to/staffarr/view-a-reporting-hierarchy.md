# How to view a reporting hierarchy

## Audience
Managers, HR, and operations leaders

## Product
StaffArr

## Support Status
Supported by current UI/API

## Purpose
See manager, team, and subordinate relationships for a person or team.

## Before You Start
- StaffArr owns reporting structure and manager relationships.
- You need permission to view the people or team records involved.

## Steps
1. Open StaffArr.
2. Open My Team for your own management view, or People for a broader search.
3. Find the person or team you need to review.
4. Open the detail view.
5. Review manager, direct reports, teams, organization unit, and location context.
6. Use filters or search if the hierarchy is large.
7. Correct missing relationships in StaffArr instead of editing a consuming product.

## What Happens Next
The hierarchy is used by readiness, permissions, approvals, assignments, and reporting.

## Troubleshooting
- If a manager relationship is missing, update the StaffArr person or team structure.
- If another product shows an old manager, refresh or investigate its StaffArr mirror/read model.
