# How to upload a photo or field evidence

## Audience
Field workers, inspectors, drivers, and technicians

## Product
Field Companion

## Support Status
Supported by current UI/API with evidence workflow

## Purpose
Attach evidence from the field to a product-owned task.

## Before You Start
- Field Companion can capture or upload evidence for supported tasks.
- RecordArr owns stored files and document metadata when evidence is retained as a record.
- Compliance Core owns evidence meaning when requirements apply.

## Steps
1. Open Field Companion.
2. Open the assigned task.
3. Find the evidence, attachment, or upload area.
4. Choose the available upload action.
5. Select the photo or file from the device.
6. Add description, evidence type, or notes if requested.
7. Submit the upload.
8. Wait for sync to complete if the device is offline or has weak connectivity.
9. Confirm the evidence appears on the task or source product record.

## What Happens Next
Field Companion sends evidence to the owning product or RecordArr-backed storage according to the task workflow.

## Troubleshooting
- If upload is unavailable, the task type may not support evidence capture.
- If upload stays pending, review connection and offline queue state before closing the task.
