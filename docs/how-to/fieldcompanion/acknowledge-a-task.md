# How to acknowledge a task

## Audience
Field workers and assigned users

## Product
Field Companion

## Support Status
Supported by current UI/API

## Purpose
Confirm you have seen or accepted a field task before performing or completing work.

## Before You Start
- The task must be visible in your Field Companion inbox.
- Acknowledgement does not complete the owning product workflow.

## Steps
1. Open Field Companion.
2. Open the field inbox.
3. Select the task.
4. Review source product, record context, due time, location, and instructions.
5. Choose the acknowledge action when available.
6. Add notes if the task asks for them.
7. Return to the inbox and confirm the task state or timestamp updated.
8. Complete the task through its product-specific workflow when work is done.

## What Happens Next
Field Companion records acknowledgement and sends it to the owning product or task service.

## Troubleshooting
- If the task cannot be acknowledged, check whether it is assigned to you, already acknowledged, completed, or blocked by permissions.
- If acknowledgement sync fails, retry after connection is restored.
