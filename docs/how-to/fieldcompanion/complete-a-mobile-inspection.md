# How to complete a mobile inspection

## Audience
Technicians, inspectors, and field workers

## Product
Field Companion

## Support Status
Supported by current UI/API

## Purpose
Complete an inspection task from a field-safe mobile surface.

## Before You Start
- The inspection must be assigned or available as a Field Companion task.
- MaintainArr owns maintenance inspections unless another product explicitly owns the inspection.

## Steps
1. Open Field Companion.
2. Open the field inbox.
3. Select the inspection task.
4. Review asset, location, due context, and instructions.
5. Answer each inspection item using the available pass, fail, numeric, or text controls.
6. Add notes or evidence when requested.
7. Save answers if the task needs to stay in progress.
8. Choose Complete inspection when all required answers are ready.
9. Review the resulting task status and any follow-up defect or work order context.

## What Happens Next
Field Companion sends inspection results to the owning product. Failed items may create or require defects, work orders, or evidence.

## Troubleshooting
- If Complete inspection is disabled, check unanswered required items or upload requirements.
- If the task fails to sync, keep the device online and review the offline queue or retry state.
