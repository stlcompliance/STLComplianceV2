# How to complete a training step from mobile

## Audience
Trainees, trainers, and field supervisors

## Product
Field Companion

## Support Status
Intended workflow not fully exposed in current Field Companion UI

## Purpose
Use a mobile task to complete a TrainArr step when mobile training tasks are available.

## Before You Start
- TrainArr owns training assignments, steps, evidence, evaluations, signoffs, and qualifications.
- Field Companion may provide the mobile surface, but it does not own the training record.
- Current Field Companion surfaces expose inspection, work order, receiving, task acknowledgement, and evidence panels; a dedicated training step panel needs confirmation.

## Steps
1. Open Field Companion.
2. Look for a TrainArr training task in the field inbox.
3. Open the task and review assignment instructions.
4. Complete the step, acknowledgement, or evidence action exposed by the task.
5. Submit the task update.
6. Open TrainArr Assignments to confirm the step or evidence was recorded.
7. Complete any trainer signoff or evaluation in TrainArr if the mobile task does not provide it.

## What Happens Next
When implemented, Field Companion should send mobile training actions back to TrainArr. TrainArr remains the source of truth.

## Troubleshooting
- If no TrainArr task appears, complete the assignment in TrainArr desktop until mobile training tasks are enabled.
- Do not treat a mobile acknowledgement as final training completion unless TrainArr shows the assignment requirement is complete.
