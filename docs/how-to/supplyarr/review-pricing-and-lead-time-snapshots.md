# How to review pricing and lead-time snapshots

## Audience
Supply chain users, planners, and purchasing managers

## Product
SupplyArr

## Support Status
Supported by current UI/API

## Purpose
Check vendor price and lead-time history without treating snapshots as financial ledger truth.

## Before You Start
- SupplyArr owns operational price and lead-time snapshots.
- External finance or ERP systems own accounting and payment execution.

## Steps
1. Open SupplyArr.
2. Open Pricing.
3. Search or filter for the vendor, part, item, or material.
4. Review the current price snapshot and any effective date or freshness information shown.
5. Review lead-time snapshot history or supplier performance context.
6. Compare snapshots before choosing a vendor or planning procurement.
7. Open related catalog or vendor records when you need source context.
8. Use ReportArr for broader trend or KPI reporting if available.

## What Happens Next
Snapshots support operational planning and procurement decisions. They are not a substitute for invoices, bills, or accounting records.

## Troubleshooting
- If a snapshot looks stale, check the last update or source mapping before using it for planning.
- If a price needs accounting approval, follow the external finance or ERP process.
