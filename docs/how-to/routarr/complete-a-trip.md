# How to complete a trip

## Audience
Drivers, dispatchers, and transportation supervisors

## Product
RoutArr

## Support Status
Supported by current UI/API with proof-review surface

## Purpose
Close transportation execution with stop completion, proof, and exception review.

## Before You Start
- RoutArr owns trip completion.
- RecordArr owns stored proof documents and retained records.
- LoadArr owns warehouse receiving or loadout state.

## Steps
1. Open RoutArr.
2. Open Trips, Driver portal, or Proof review.
3. Select the trip.
4. Confirm all required stops are completed or exceptions are recorded.
5. Add proof of pickup or proof of delivery when required.
6. Resolve damaged freight, missed stop, or validation blockers.
7. Use the complete or close action when available.
8. Review final trip status, proof, and audit trail.
9. Check RecordArr or ReportArr for audit packages or performance reports when needed.

## What Happens Next
RoutArr records the completed trip and transportation evidence references. RecordArr can store proof documents for retention and audit.

## Troubleshooting
- If completion is blocked, check missing stop proof, unresolved exceptions, or validation blockers.
- If proof upload is not available in RoutArr, attach the document through RecordArr and reference the trip.
