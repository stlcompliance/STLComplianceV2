# How to update trip status

## Audience
Drivers, dispatchers, and transportation supervisors

## Product
RoutArr

## Support Status
Supported by current UI/API with driver portal surface

## Purpose
Keep the trip current as work progresses from planned to active, delayed, completed, or exception state.

## Before You Start
- RoutArr owns trip status and transportation exceptions.
- Field Companion or Driver portal may provide mobile execution surfaces.

## Steps
1. Open RoutArr.
2. Open Trips, Dispatch board, Driver portal, or Load visibility.
3. Select the trip.
4. Review route, stops, driver, equipment, load readiness, and blockers.
5. Use the available status update action for the current trip state.
6. Enter notes, timing, proof, or exception details requested by the page.
7. Save the status update.
8. Confirm downstream visibility or customer portal state when applicable.

## What Happens Next
RoutArr records trip status and may publish transportation events for LoadArr, RecordArr, ReportArr, or customer-facing views.

## Troubleshooting
- If a status update is blocked, review validation blockers and required proof fields.
- If the issue is load, vehicle, or driver readiness, correct it in the owning product.
