# How to complete a training step

## Audience
Trainees, trainers, and training admins

## Product
TrainArr

## Support Status
Supported by current UI/API

## Purpose
Mark required training steps complete and provide evidence when required.

## Before You Start
- You need access to the assignment.
- Some assignments require evidence, evaluation, or signoff before final completion.

## Steps
1. Open TrainArr.
2. Open Assignments.
3. Select the assignment.
4. Review Assignment detail to understand status, evidence count, and completion requirements.
5. Open the steps panel.
6. Complete the required step actions.
7. Upload evidence if the assignment requires proof.
8. Review whether evaluation and signoffs are still required.
9. Use Mark complete only when the assignment says completion requirements are met.

## What Happens Next
TrainArr updates the assignment record and can publish qualification or readiness results when completion changes status.

## Troubleshooting
- If Mark complete is disabled, check missing evaluation, trainee signoff, trainer signoff, or evidence.
- If evidence upload is unavailable, verify assignment access and evidence permissions.
