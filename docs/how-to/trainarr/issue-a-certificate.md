# How to issue a certificate

## Audience
Training admins and qualification managers

## Product
TrainArr

## Support Status
Intended workflow partially supported by current routes/docs

## Purpose
Issue or publish a qualification after the required training and signoffs are complete.

## Before You Start
- TrainArr owns qualification issuance and lifecycle events.
- StaffArr may display the qualification after TrainArr publishes it.
- The current assignment detail can show qualification issue information, but final issue controls should be confirmed.

## Steps
1. Open TrainArr.
2. Open Assignments or Qualifications.
3. Find the completed assignment or qualified person.
4. Verify required steps, evaluation, signoffs, and evidence are complete.
5. Use the qualification or certificate issue action when available.
6. Set issue, expiration, renewal, or status details requested by the page.
7. Save or publish the qualification.
8. Confirm StaffArr readiness reflects the updated qualification.

## What Happens Next
TrainArr records the certificate or qualification lifecycle and publishes the result for StaffArr and other products to consume.

## Troubleshooting
- If the issue action is not visible, confirm the tenant has the qualification lifecycle UI enabled.
- Do not manually mark StaffArr as qualified without TrainArr owning the qualification.
