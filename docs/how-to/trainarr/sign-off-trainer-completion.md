# How to sign off trainer completion

## Audience
Trainers and training admins

## Product
TrainArr

## Support Status
Supported by current UI/API

## Purpose
Record trainer approval for an assignment that requires trainer signoff.

## Before You Start
- The assignment must exist and be visible to the trainer or authorized admin.
- Trainer signoff is separate from trainee acknowledgement and practical evaluation.

## Steps
1. Open TrainArr.
2. Open Assignments.
3. Select the assignment.
4. Review completed steps, uploaded evidence, and evaluation status.
5. Open the signoff/evaluation area.
6. Choose the trainer signoff action.
7. Enter notes or results requested by the form.
8. Submit the trainer signoff.
9. Complete the assignment only when all requirements are met.

## What Happens Next
TrainArr records trainer signoff and updates the assignment completion readiness.

## Troubleshooting
- If trainer signoff is unavailable, verify trainer permissions and assignment scope.
- If a practical evaluation is required, submit that evaluation before completing the assignment.
