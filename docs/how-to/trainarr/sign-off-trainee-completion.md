# How to sign off trainee completion

## Audience
Trainees and authorized training admins

## Product
TrainArr

## Support Status
Supported by current UI/API

## Purpose
Record the trainee signoff required by some training assignments.

## Before You Start
- The assignment must exist and be visible to the trainee or authorized user.
- The trainee signoff action is permission-gated.

## Steps
1. Open TrainArr.
2. Open Assignments.
3. Select the assignment.
4. Review Assignment detail and the signoff/evaluation area.
5. Confirm required steps and evidence are complete enough for trainee acknowledgement.
6. Choose the trainee signoff action.
7. Enter any acknowledgement notes requested by the form.
8. Submit the signoff.
9. Review the assignment readiness message to see whether trainer signoff, evaluation, or final completion is still required.

## What Happens Next
TrainArr records the trainee signoff on the assignment. It does not complete the assignment unless all completion requirements are met.

## Troubleshooting
- If the signoff action is unavailable, confirm you are the trainee or have the required training admin permission.
- If the assignment still cannot complete, review the evaluation and trainer signoff requirements.
