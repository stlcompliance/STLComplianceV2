# How to review expiring certifications

## Audience
Training admins, compliance managers, and supervisors

## Product
TrainArr

## Support Status
Supported by current UI/API with intended renewal workflow

## Purpose
Find certifications or qualifications that are close to expiration and plan renewal work.

## Before You Start
- TrainArr owns certification expiration and renewal status.
- StaffArr may display readiness impact.

## Steps
1. Open TrainArr.
2. Open Qualifications or Dashboard.
3. Filter for expiring, expired, or renewal-needed qualifications when the page exposes those filters.
4. Open the affected person or qualification detail.
5. Review the renewal requirement, due date, and evidence state.
6. Create or review the renewal assignment in Assignments or Queue.
7. Follow up with the trainee, trainer, or manager.
8. Confirm StaffArr readiness after renewal completion.

## What Happens Next
TrainArr remains the owner of qualification status and renewal evidence.

## Troubleshooting
- If the expiring filter is missing, use Qualifications or Dashboard views to search by person or qualification until the renewal queue is fully exposed.
- If StaffArr shows stale status, verify TrainArr publication or sync state.
