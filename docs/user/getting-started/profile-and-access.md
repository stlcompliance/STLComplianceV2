# Profile, Tenant, and Permissions

## Steps
1. Open the user menu or StaffArr **My profile**.
2. Confirm your identity and current tenant.
3. Review your position, team, assignments, permissions, readiness, restrictions, and training/qualification snapshots where available.
4. Open any ordinary product from the switcher.
5. When an action is unavailable, record the product, page, record, and action for your administrator.

Product availability and product authority are different: active tenant members can open ordinary products; product permissions and workflow rules decide what they can do.
