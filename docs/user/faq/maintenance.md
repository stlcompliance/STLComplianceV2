# Maintenance FAQ

## Who owns assets and work orders?
MaintainArr owns assets, defects, inspections, PM programs, work orders, repairs, downtime, and maintenance evidence.

## Who owns parts inventory?
LoadArr owns stock movement and balances. SupplyArr owns item and vendor context.

## Why can I not close a work order?
You may lack close access, blockers may remain, or evidence/review may be incomplete.
