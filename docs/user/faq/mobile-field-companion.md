# Mobile Field Companion FAQ

## Does Field Companion own the task?
No. Field Companion shows and captures field work. The source product owns the task and final record.

## What does Acknowledge do?
It confirms you saw the task. It does not complete the underlying work unless the task explicitly says so.

## What happens offline?
Pending actions stay in the Offline queue until they sync. Do not assume unsynced actions are confirmed.
