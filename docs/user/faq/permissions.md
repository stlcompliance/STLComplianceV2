# Permissions FAQ

## What is the difference between role and permission?
A role groups permissions. A permission controls a specific action such as read, create, approve, execute, export, hold, or release.

## Why did a role change not work immediately?
Permission projection may need to refresh. Ask a StaffArr admin to check projection history.

## Can an admin bypass approvals?
Approvals and blockers should not be bypassed unless the owning product has an explicit override workflow.
