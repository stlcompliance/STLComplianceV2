# Account Access FAQ

## Why can I sign in but see no products?
Your account may be valid, but product entitlement is missing. Ask a tenant admin or platform admin to check NexArr access.

## Why can I open a product but not click an action?
Product entitlement controls launch. Product permissions control actions inside the product.

## Who changes my profile information?
StaffArr admins manage person records. NexArr/platform admins manage platform account and launch access.
