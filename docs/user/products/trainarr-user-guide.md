# TrainArr User Guide

## What This Product Is For
TrainArr is for training programs, modules, steps, assignments, evaluations, trainee signoffs, trainer signoffs, remediation, qualification rules, certifications, and qualification publication to StaffArr.

## Who Uses It
- trainers
- trainees
- managers
- compliance users

## Main Pages
- Dashboard
- Programs
- Assignments
- Remediation
- Citations
- Rule packs
- Qualifications
- Settings

## Main Records
- training program
- training step
- training assignment
- training evidence
- evaluation
- trainee signoff
- trainer signoff
- qualification issue

## Common Workflows
- build programs
- assign training
- upload evidence
- submit evaluations
- complete trainee and trainer signoffs
- publish qualification results to StaffArr

## Permissions Usually Needed
- trainee
- trainer
- tenant_admin
- training admin or manager roles where configured

## Related Products
- StaffArr owns the person record.
- Compliance Core may define evidence requirements.
- RecordArr stores retained training evidence when used as records.

## Common Troubleshooting
- [Product not visible](../troubleshooting/product-not-visible.md)
- [Missing permission](../troubleshooting/missing-permission.md)
- If a page is visible but an action is disabled, check the record status and your role or permission assignment.
- Remember: TrainArr does not own person master records, job or position master records, incident master records, maintenance work, dispatch work, inventory work, payroll, or accounting.
