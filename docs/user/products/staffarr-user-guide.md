# StaffArr User Guide

## What This Product Is For
StaffArr is for people, workers, org units, internal sites, locations, role assignments, permission assignments, readiness, restrictions, and personnel incident history.

## Who Uses It
- StaffArr admins
- HR users
- managers
- supervisors
- trainers who need person context

## Main Pages
- My profile
- My team
- People
- Org structure
- Locations
- Roles
- Readiness
- Restrictions
- Incidents
- Training acks
- Certifications
- Settings
- Admin

## Main Records
- person
- org unit
- site
- location
- role template
- permission assignment
- readiness restriction
- personnel incident
- certification snapshot

## Common Workflows
- create people
- place people in org units
- assign roles
- review permission projection history
- report personnel incidents
- route training-compliance incidents to TrainArr

## Permissions Usually Needed
- staffarr.incidents.manage
- tenant_admin
- staffarr_admin
- hr_admin
- supervisor

## Related Products
- NexArr validates identity, tenant membership, session, and launch context; StaffArr supplies authority context and products enforce their actions.
- TrainArr publishes qualification and certification status to StaffArr.
- Other products reference StaffArr people and locations.

## Common Troubleshooting
- [Product or feature not visible](../troubleshooting/product-or-feature-not-visible.md)
- [Missing permission](../troubleshooting/missing-permission.md)
- If a page is visible but an action is disabled, check the record status and your role or permission assignment.
- Remember: StaffArr does not own training programs, certification issuance, maintenance work, dispatch work, inventory work, payroll, or accounting.
