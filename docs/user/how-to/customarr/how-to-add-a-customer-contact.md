# How to add a customer contact

## Audience
Customer operations users and account managers.

## Purpose
Add an external customer contact and record what the contact is authorized to do.

## Before You Start
- CustomArr access.
- Customer account selected.
- Contact name, communication details, role, and authorization scope.

## Steps
1. Open CustomArr.
2. Open the customer account.
3. Open Contacts.
4. Add the contact details.
5. Add or update the contact authorization record for actions such as requesting service, approving waivers, receiving notices, or signing proof of delivery.
6. Save the contact and review the authorization summary.

## What Happens Next
CustomArr owns customer contacts and contact authorization records. The contact is not a StaffArr worker unless the tenant explicitly converts or links that person through the correct identity workflow.

## Troubleshooting
- If authorization options are missing, check `customarr.contacts.manage`.
- If the contact needs portal login, use the portal access workflow so NexArr remains the login and trust authority.
- If the person is a supplier contact, use SupplyArr instead.
