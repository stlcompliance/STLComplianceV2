# How to complete a trip

## Audience
Drivers, dispatchers, and RoutArr managers.

## Purpose
Finish a trip after required stops, proof, and blockers are resolved.

## Before You Start
- RoutArr access.
- Trip execution access.
- Assigned driver and equipment where required.
- Proof of pickup or delivery if required.

## Steps
1. Open RoutArr.
2. Open **Trips** or **Driver portal**.
3. Select the trip.
4. Confirm all required stops are completed or exceptions are recorded.
5. Submit proof records where required.
6. Resolve validation blockers.
7. Use the completion action or final status control shown by the page.
8. Review **Proof review** if the trip needs verification.

## What Happens Next
RoutArr records the trip completion. Closeout may still require proof review, evidence verification, or reporting.

## Troubleshooting
- If completion is blocked, review driver assignment, equipment assignment, proof, and validation blockers.
- If proof is rejected, correct or resubmit it according to the proof review workflow.
- If load status is wrong, check LoadArr load visibility.
