# How to stage received items

## Audience
Warehouse receivers and inventory staff.

## Purpose
Place received items into a staging state before putaway, shipping, or handoff.

## Before You Start
- LoadArr access.
- A received item or receiving session.
- A staging location or assignment.

## Steps
1. Open LoadArr.
2. Open **Work** > **Staging** or the receiving session detail.
3. Select the received item or staging assignment.
4. Confirm the item, quantity, and source location.
5. Choose the staging area if the field is shown.
6. Save or complete the staging action.
7. Review **Inventory** or **Movement History** to confirm movement.

## What Happens Next
LoadArr records the staging movement. The item may later be put away, picked, loaded, or transferred.

## Troubleshooting
- If the staging location is missing, check StaffArr and LoadArr setup.
- If quantity is unavailable, review receiving completion and holds.
- If the item is quarantined, release it only through the hold or quarantine workflow.
