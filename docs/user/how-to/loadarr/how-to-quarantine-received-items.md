# How to quarantine received items

## Audience
Warehouse receivers, supervisors, and quality users.

## Purpose
Place received items into quarantine when condition, compliance, or quality requires review.

## Before You Start
- LoadArr access.
- Permission to place inventory hold or create exceptions.
- Receiving session, item, quantity, and reason.

## Steps
1. Open LoadArr.
2. Open **Receiving** or **Exceptions**.
3. Select the item or exception.
4. Choose a condition or resolution that indicates quarantine when available.
5. Enter the reason and notes.
6. Save the hold or exception update.
7. Confirm the item appears under **Holds**, **Exceptions**, or the quarantine filter.

## What Happens Next
LoadArr blocks normal availability for quarantined stock until release or other resolution.

## Troubleshooting
- If quarantine option is missing, use a receiving exception and contact a supervisor.
- If the item still appears available, check hold status and stock ledger.
- If quality review is needed, escalate through the configured quality workflow.
