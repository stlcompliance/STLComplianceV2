# How to transfer inventory between locations

## Audience
Inventory staff and warehouse managers.

## Purpose
Move stock between locations while preserving LoadArr stock movement history.

## Before You Start
- LoadArr access.
- Transfer create and execute permission as needed.
- Item, quantity, source location, and destination location.

## Steps
1. Open LoadArr.
2. Open **Work** > **Transfers**.
3. Select **new** transfer if available.
4. Choose transfer type such as Bin to bin or Zone to zone where shown.
5. Choose source and destination locations.
6. Choose item and quantity.
7. Save the transfer.
8. Execute or complete the transfer after physical movement.
9. Review **Movement History**.

## What Happens Next
LoadArr updates the movement history and balance records.

## Troubleshooting
- If stock cannot be transferred, check reservations, holds, and available quantity.
- If locations are missing, check StaffArr location identity and LoadArr setup.
- If execution is disabled, check permission and transfer status.
