# How to report an incident

## Audience
StaffArr admins, HR users, or authorized incident reporters.

## Purpose
Create a StaffArr personnel incident tied to a person.

## Before You Start
- StaffArr access.
- Incident manage access such as tenant_admin, staffarr_admin, or hr_admin.
- The person record.
- Incident title, description, reason category, severity, and occurred time.

## Steps
1. Open StaffArr.
2. Open **Incidents** and select **Create**, or open a person and select **Create incident** in the incidents area.
3. Choose the person.
4. Choose a reason category: Safety, Conduct, Injury, Equipment, Training compliance, Policy, or Other.
5. Choose severity: Low, Medium, High, or Critical.
6. Enter the title and description.
7. Confirm the occurred time.
8. Submit the incident.
9. Add notes or attachments if needed and available.

## What Happens Next
StaffArr creates an open personnel incident. Training compliance incidents may be routed to TrainArr when the routing action is available.

## Troubleshooting
- If **Create incident** is missing, check incident manage access.
- If the person is missing, check the People directory and filters.
- If TrainArr routing is unavailable, confirm the category is Training compliance and the integration is enabled.
