# How to deactivate a person

## Audience
StaffArr admins or HR users managing person lifecycle.

## Purpose
Remove a person from normal active use while preserving their history.

## Before You Start
- StaffArr access.
- Permission to manage people.
- The person record.
- A reason and effective date if required by your process.

## Steps
1. Open StaffArr.
2. Open **People** and select the person.
3. Open the profile editor or offboarding area.
4. Change Employment status to **Inactive**, **Terminated**, **Suspended**, or **Archived** as appropriate.
5. Remove or expire role assignments that should no longer apply.
6. Save the person record.
7. Review readiness, restrictions, and access summaries.

## What Happens Next
The person remains in StaffArr history but should stop appearing in active operational choices where the product filters inactive people.

## Troubleshooting
- Do not delete a person to end access; use lifecycle status and role removal.
- If the user should no longer sign in, check NexArr account state, tenant membership, and active sessions.
- If the person still appears in another product, check that product reference sync has refreshed.
