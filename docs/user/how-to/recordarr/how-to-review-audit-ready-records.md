# How to review audit-ready records

## Audience
Compliance users, records users, and auditors.

## Purpose
Review records and packages that may support an audit.

## Before You Start
- RecordArr sign-in and permissions.
- Record package or audit scope.
- Permission to view sensitive records.

## Steps
1. Open RecordArr.
2. Open **Packages** or **Record Packages**.
3. Select the package.
4. Review included records, status, created date, completed date, locked date, archived date, and expiration date.
5. Open individual records for metadata, comments, access logs, and evidence mappings.
6. Check **Retention**, **Holds**, or **Legal Holds** if audit retention is involved.
7. Export or share only through approved actions.

## What Happens Next
The package can help support audit review. Review findings with your compliance lead.

## Troubleshooting
- If a record is missing from the package, confirm it was attached or mapped.
- If access is denied, ask a RecordArr administrator to review your permissions or access policy.
- If a legal hold applies, do not dispose of the record.
