# How to attach a document to a record

## Audience
Product users and records users attaching support files.

## Purpose
Connect a stored document to a business or evidence record.

## Before You Start
- RecordArr sign-in and document permissions, or owning product attachment permission.
- Stored document or upload session.
- Related business record.

## Steps
1. Open the owning product record or RecordArr **Records**.
2. Find the attachment, link, or evidence mapping area.
3. Choose the document or upload it if needed.
4. Select the relationship type if shown.
5. Save the link or mapping.
6. Review the record detail to confirm the document appears.

## What Happens Next
The owning product keeps the business record. RecordArr keeps the document and access history.

## Troubleshooting
- If the document is missing, upload it first.
- If the link action is missing, check permission.
- If compliance evidence is needed, use the evidence mapping area or ask your compliance lead.
