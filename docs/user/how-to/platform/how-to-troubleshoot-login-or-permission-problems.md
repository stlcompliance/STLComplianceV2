# How to Troubleshoot Login, Launch, or Permission Problems

1. Check account state, lock, MFA, and tenant membership in NexArr.
2. For a missing ordinary product, check launcher registry, operational state, cache, and handoff configuration.
3. For the Compliance Core studio, confirm platform-admin status.
4. If the product opens, check StaffArr permissions and scope.
5. Check product workflow state, qualifications, holds, and blockers.
6. Capture the visible error, product, page, record, action, and time for support.
