# Platform / How To Use Preferences

Preferences are personal defaults. They are not tenant settings, product settings, admin settings, or compliance rules.

## Where To Find Them

- Open the avatar or display-name menu in the top bar.
- Choose `Preferences`.
- Use the full page to change suite-wide and current-product defaults.

## What Belongs In Preferences

- Suite-wide appearance settings such as theme, density, motion, contrast, and formatting
- Current-product workflow defaults such as launch or home-view preferences

## What Does Not Belong In Preferences

- Tenant policy
- Compliance rules
- Required approvals
- Permissions
- Retention settings
- Product ownership rules

## Why Theme Is Suite-Wide

Theme follows the person across STL Compliance products, so it is stored and applied at the suite level instead of being duplicated per app.

## Why Workflow Defaults Stay Product-Local

Workflow defaults only affect the current product. That keeps one product from silently changing how another product behaves.
