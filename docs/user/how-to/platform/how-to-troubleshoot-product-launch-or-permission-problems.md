# How to Troubleshoot a Product Launch or Permission Problem

1. Confirm the correct tenant is selected.
2. Refresh the shared shell and retry the ordinary product.
3. Ask support to verify product registry/operational state and launch handoff when it is missing or fails.
4. Remember that Compliance Core's administrative studio is visible only to platform administrators.
5. When the product opens but an action is missing, ask a StaffArr or product administrator to review permissions, scope, workflow state, qualifications, and blockers.
