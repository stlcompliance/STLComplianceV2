# How to troubleshoot missing product access

## Audience
Users and tenant administrators.

## Purpose
Find the likely reason a product is missing or cannot launch.

## Before You Start
- The user can sign in.
- The expected tenant is selected.
- Admin access if you need to change entitlement.

## Steps
1. Ask the user which product is missing.
2. Confirm the product is entitled for the tenant.
3. Confirm the user account has the product in its allowed product list.
4. Ask the user to refresh and open the product switcher again.
5. If launch fails, have a support contact review launch details.
6. If the product opens but actions are missing, review product permissions instead of entitlement.

## What Happens Next
When entitlement and launch are fixed, the product should appear in the switcher. Product actions may still require product-specific permissions.

## Troubleshooting
- No entitled products means entitlement is missing or not returned for the user.
- Launch errors may indicate a stale handoff, disabled product, or configuration issue.
- Product permissions are usually managed by StaffArr or the owning product, not by the switcher.
