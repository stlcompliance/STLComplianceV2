# A product is not visible

## Symptoms
- Product switcher does not show the expected product.
- Switcher says **No entitled products**.
- Product launch fails.

## Likely Causes
- Tenant is not entitled to the product.
- User account is not allowed to launch it.
- Product is disabled or launch handoff failed.

## What to Check
1. Confirm you are in the correct tenant.
2. Ask an admin to check product entitlement.
3. Ask an admin to check your user access.
4. For launch failure, ask a support contact to review launch details.

## How to Fix
- Add or repair product entitlement.
- Update user access.
- Refresh and try product switcher again.
- Resolve launch configuration issue.

## Who Can Help
Tenant administrator or support contact.
