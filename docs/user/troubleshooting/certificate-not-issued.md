# A certificate was not issued

## Symptoms
- Assignment appears complete but no qualification or certification appears.
- StaffArr Certifications does not show the expected result.

## Likely Causes
- Evaluation is missing or not pass.
- Trainee signoff missing.
- Trainer signoff missing.
- Publication to StaffArr not complete.
- Certificate issuance workflow is partially available.

## What to Check
1. Open TrainArr assignment.
2. Review Evaluation & signoffs.
3. Confirm pass result.
4. Confirm trainee and trainer signoffs.
5. Open Qualifications and StaffArr Certifications.

## How to Fix
- Submit missing evaluation or signoff.
- Complete the assignment using the available action.
- Ask a TrainArr admin to review qualification issue history.
- Check publication settings.

## Who Can Help
Trainer or TrainArr admin.
