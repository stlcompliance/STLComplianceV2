# A Product, Page, or Action Is Not Visible

## Symptoms
- An ordinary product is missing from the product switcher.
- A product launch fails.
- The product opens but a page, button, or record is missing.

## What to check
1. Confirm the current tenant.
2. For a missing ordinary product, refresh the suite and ask support to check product registry, operational status, shell cache, and launch configuration.
3. For Compliance Core, remember the administrative studio is platform-admin-only.
4. For a missing page/action, ask a StaffArr or product administrator to review effective permissions and scope.
5. For a missing record, check filters, ownership, tenant, record scope, archive state, and degraded source dependencies.
6. For a disabled action, check workflow state, qualifications, holds, blockers, and approval rules.

## Who can help
Platform support for login/launch; StaffArr or product administrators for permissions and scope; the owning product team for workflow/data issues.
