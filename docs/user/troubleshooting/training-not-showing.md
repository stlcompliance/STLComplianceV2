# Training is not showing

## Symptoms
- Training assignment is missing.
- TrainArr task does not appear in Field Companion.
- StaffArr does not show training acknowledgement.

## Likely Causes
- Assignment was not created.
- Person record mismatch.
- Required assignment, permission, or source task is missing.
- Publication or inbox sync has not refreshed.

## What to Check
1. Open TrainArr Assignments.
2. Confirm StaffArr person.
3. Check Field Companion product permissions.
4. Check StaffArr Training acks or Certifications.

## How to Fix
- Create or correct the assignment.
- Fix person linkage.
- Refresh product or inbox.
- Ask admin to check publication settings.

## Who Can Help
Trainer, TrainArr admin, or StaffArr admin.
