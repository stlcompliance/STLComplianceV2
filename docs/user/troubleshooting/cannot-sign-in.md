# I cannot sign in

## Symptoms
- Sign-in page rejects your username or password.
- Verification code fails.
- You return to the sign-in page.

## Likely Causes
- Wrong credentials.
- Account is inactive or not created.
- Verification method is unavailable.
- Session expired.

## What to Check
1. Confirm the sign-in URL.
2. Confirm username or email.
3. Try the verification flow again.
4. Ask an admin to confirm your account exists and is active.

## How to Fix
- Reset your password if available.
- Ask a tenant administrator or support contact to check your user account.
- Clear stale browser session and sign in again.

## Who Can Help
Tenant administrator or support contact.
