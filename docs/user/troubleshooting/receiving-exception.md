# There is a receiving exception

## Symptoms
- Received quantity does not match expected quantity.
- Item is damaged or quarantined.
- Receiving session cannot complete cleanly.

## Likely Causes
- Shortage or overage.
- Damage.
- Compliance or quality review required.
- Wrong item or location.

## What to Check
1. Open LoadArr Receiving.
2. Review expected and received quantities.
3. Check condition and exception reason.
4. Check Holds or Quarantine.

## How to Fix
- Create or update the receiving exception.
- Quarantine or hold affected stock if required.
- Resolve as valid stock, scrap, or review according to allowed actions.
- Escalate quality-bearing issues.

## Who Can Help
Warehouse supervisor, LoadArr admin, or quality/compliance lead.
