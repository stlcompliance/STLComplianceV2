# Audit Readiness Overview

Audit readiness means users can find the records, evidence, source context, access history, and reports needed to support review.

## What to review
- Operational records in the owning product.
- Stored documents and packages in RecordArr.
- Rule and evidence meaning in Compliance Core.
- Reports and exports in ReportArr.
- Activity and access history where available.

## Practical steps
1. Identify the audit scope.
2. Find source records in the owning product.
3. Find retained files and packages in RecordArr.
4. Review evidence requirements in Compliance Core.
5. Generate or export reports in ReportArr if needed.
6. Check retention and legal hold before disposal or sharing.

This guide does not provide legal advice. Use these records to help support review, and confirm conclusions with your compliance lead.
