# Training Evidence

Training evidence may include assignments, evidence uploads, evaluations, trainee signoffs, trainer signoffs, qualification issues, certification snapshots, and retained documents.

## Where to look
- TrainArr Assignments for assignment status, evidence, evaluation, and signoffs.
- TrainArr Qualifications for qualification issue status and history.
- StaffArr Certifications for published snapshots.
- RecordArr for retained certificates, uploaded files, or packages.

This guide does not provide legal advice. Use these records to help support review, and confirm conclusions with your compliance lead.
