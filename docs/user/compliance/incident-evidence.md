# Incident Evidence

Incident evidence may include StaffArr personnel incident records, notes, attachments, severity, category, status, related TrainArr retraining assignments, and retained documents.

## Where to look
- StaffArr Incidents or a person's Personnel incidents area.
- TrainArr Assignments or Remediation when retraining is routed.
- RecordArr for retained attachments.
- ReportArr for incident trend reports where configured.

This guide does not provide legal advice. Use these records to help support review, and confirm conclusions with your compliance lead.
