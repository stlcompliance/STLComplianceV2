# Rule and Citation Basics

Rules, citations, and requirements explain why evidence is needed and which records help support review.

## Practical reminders
- A citation explains where a requirement comes from.
- A rule or requirement explains what must be true or what evidence may be needed.
- A product record shows what actually happened operationally.
- RecordArr stores retained evidence files and access history.
- If rule matches are missing, check facts, mappings, rulepack status, and evidence links.

This guide does not provide legal advice. Use these records to help support review, and confirm conclusions with your compliance lead.
