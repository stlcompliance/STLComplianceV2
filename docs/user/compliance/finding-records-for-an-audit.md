# Finding Records for an Audit

Start with the product that owns the work, then use RecordArr and ReportArr to gather retained records and summaries.

## Steps
1. Identify the business process: training, maintenance, receiving, dispatch, procurement, records, or compliance.
2. Open the owning product and find the source record.
3. Open RecordArr and search for linked files, evidence mappings, packages, access logs, holds, or retention records.
4. Open Compliance Core if the audit question depends on a rule, citation, requirement, or evidence requirement.
5. Open ReportArr if you need a dashboard, report run, export, KPI, or audit package.

This guide does not provide legal advice. Use these records to help support review, and confirm conclusions with your compliance lead.
