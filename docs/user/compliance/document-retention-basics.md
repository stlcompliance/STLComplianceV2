# Document Retention Basics

RecordArr owns retained stored files, document metadata, versions, retention schedules, legal holds, document approvals, acknowledgements, and access history.

## Basic rules
- Do not delete records that are under legal hold.
- Archived is not deleted.
- Superseded records should remain available for historical decisions.
- Access history matters for sensitive or controlled records.
- If a report export becomes a retained record, store it through RecordArr.

This guide does not provide legal advice. Use these records to help support review, and confirm conclusions with your compliance lead.
