# Maintenance Evidence

Maintenance evidence may include assets, defects, work orders, inspection runs, labor, evidence uploads, parts demand, downtime, and readiness history.

## Where to look
- MaintainArr Asset registry, Work orders, Defects, Inspection runs, Maintenance history, and Downtime.
- LoadArr when inventory movement, holds, or parts availability matter.
- SupplyArr when procurement context matters.
- RecordArr for retained files and packages.

This guide does not provide legal advice. Use these records to help support review, and confirm conclusions with your compliance lead.
