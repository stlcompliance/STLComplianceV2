# Hire to Qualified

## Purpose
Move a new person from StaffArr setup through TrainArr assignment and qualification visibility.

## Who Participates
- StaffArr admin
- Trainer
- Manager
- Trainee

## Starting Point
A person needs to be created or onboarded in StaffArr.

## Main Steps
1. Create the person in StaffArr.
2. Place the person in site, department, team, and position.
3. Assign needed role templates.
4. Assign training in TrainArr.
5. Complete evidence, evaluation, trainee signoff, and trainer signoff.
6. Review qualification or certification status in TrainArr and StaffArr.

## Products Involved
- StaffArr owns person and role context.
- TrainArr owns training and qualification.
- RecordArr may store retained evidence.

## Records Created or Updated
- person
- role assignment
- training assignment
- evaluation
- signoff
- qualification or certification snapshot

## Where Users May Get Stuck
- Person missing from TrainArr because StaffArr setup is incomplete.
- Training assignment blocked by missing program.
- Certificate not issued because evaluation or signoff is missing.
