# Trainer Guide

## What This Role Does
A trainer builds or runs training assignments, submits trainer signoffs, reviews evidence, and helps move assignments toward qualification completion.

## What This Role Can Usually Access
- TrainArr Programs and Assignments when entitled and assigned.
- Evaluation and signoff panels when the role allows it.
- StaffArr person and certification context when separately permitted.

## What This Role Usually Cannot Access
- Does not own person master records; StaffArr owns people.
- Cannot issue or revoke certifications unless TrainArr permissions allow it.
- Cannot close incidents; StaffArr owns personnel incident history.

## Common Daily Tasks
- Create or update training programs where allowed.
- Assign training.
- Review evidence.
- Submit evaluation results.
- Select **Trainer signoff** when trainer signoff is required and available.

## Records This Role Works With
- training program
- training assignment
- training evidence
- evaluation
- trainer signoff
- qualification issue

## Notifications This Role May Receive
- Training assignments needing evaluation
- signoff tasks
- remediation or retraining tasks

## Common Issues
- Completion is blocked until there is a passing evaluation plus trainee and trainer signoffs.
- Trainee signoff is only available to the trainee or permitted role.
- Training is not visible if assignment or product access is missing.
